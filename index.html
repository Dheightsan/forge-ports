Licenses & rights

I only port what I have rights to port

Most devs in this space ignore licensing. I don't — because a DMCA takedown or account ban hurts you, not just me.

🌐 Public commissions

Open-source mods (MIT · Apache · LGPL) or mods with written author permission. I port and publish to CurseForge & Modrinth with full credit to the original author.

🔒 Private commissions

You bring a mod you have the right to use. I port it and deliver privately — only to you. You decide if and how to distribute it. I never publish it without your instruction.

✋ What I won't do

Publicly redistribute All Rights Reserved mods without written author permission. This protects your accounts on CurseForge, Modrinth and BuiltByBit from takedowns and bans.

The principle: You pay for my time writing the port — not for distribution rights. The right to publish always belongs to whoever holds the license. That's the only clean, legally sound way to run a porting service.
